FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about generating stable XPaths.

Does XPathFinder store my HTML?

No. Processing happens in your browser, and no HTML is stored by XPathFinder.

Why do I see different results for some URLs?

Some websites render content with JavaScript. Use the Playwright backend for full DOM capture.

What makes an XPath stable?

Stable XPaths rely on unique attributes such as IDs, test IDs, or accessibility labels instead of position.

Can I use this for Selenium or Playwright?

Yes. The generated XPaths work directly in both Selenium and Playwright.
